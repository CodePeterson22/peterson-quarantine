Sports

High school sports at the Freehold Boro is amazing. I play a winter sport (basketball) and a spring sport (volleyball). When I am not on the court I love watching and supporting fellow colonial peers.

Friends

I am so happy that I have met so many new individuals over these two years. Kids from all different towns (Howell, Marlboro, Manalapan, etc). It has been amazing making new friendships and I can't wait to meet more people in the next two years.

FRIENDS

Freehold Boro High School

Freehold Boro has offered me so many new opportunities and has unlocked so many different abilities that I didn't know I have. All the clubs and extra-cirricular you can do really helps develop you as a person and allows you to get see who you really are as a person.

BORO

Battle of the Classes

Battle of the Classes (BOTC) is a great way to show Boro pride. The whole week of BOTC is amazing from all the school spirit. Especially the day of the BOTC, the dance and all the events that they have to do are so fun to watch. This one event really brings the school together as each grade level cheers for their class. I'm sad it got cancelled this year :(
